## Muted Palettes

Before 2015, sneakers were neon. They were bright red, electric blue, and volt yellow. Then came the "Yeezy Season" aesthetic.

Suddenly, everyone wanted:
* Olive Green
* Sand
* Clay
* Bone

It changed how we dress. We stopped trying to stand out and started trying to blend in.
