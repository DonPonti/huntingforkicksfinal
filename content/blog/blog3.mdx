## Raw Materials

We are seeing a shift away from "clean" design to "raw" design. Exposed foam, unfinished seams, and heavy, industrial silhouettes.

Brands like **Rick Owens** and **Balenciaga** paved the way, but now we see it in mainstream releases. The shoe looks like a building. It looks like it was cast in concrete.
